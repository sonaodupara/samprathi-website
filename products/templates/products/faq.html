Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about shopping with Samprathi.

Orders & Shopping

Browse our collection, select your preferred saree, add it to your cart, proceed to checkout, enter your delivery details, and confirm your order.

Shipping & Delivery

Orders are processed within 1–3 business days. Delivery usually takes 5–10 working days depending on your location within India.

Once shipped, tracking details are sent via email/SMS. You can also view real-time status on the Shipment Tracking page.

Returns & Exchanges

Returns are accepted only for damaged or defective products received during delivery. Due to the handcrafted nature of our sarees, we do not accept returns for change of mind, colour variation, or fit expectations.

Please record an unboxing video while opening the package (damage must be clearly visible). Contact us within 24 hours of delivery with photos and video. Returns without unboxing proof cannot be processed.

Exchanges are not available at this time. Please refer to size charts and product descriptions carefully before ordering.
